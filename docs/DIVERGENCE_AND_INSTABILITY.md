# Divergence and Instability

This document defines the public intuition behind OMNIA-THREE-BODY signal classes.

---

## Divergence

Divergence means that nearby trajectories separate under evolution.

Public intuition:

    small initial difference
    larger later difference

Divergence is expected in chaotic systems.

The point is not that divergence exists.

The point is to observe how structural behavior changes as divergence appears.

---

## Perturbation sensitivity

Perturbation sensitivity means that small controlled changes can produce large downstream differences.

Public intuition:

    the system amplifies small changes

This makes three-body style dynamics useful as a stress test.

---

## Structural instability

Structural instability means that a measured or observed structural property changes, degrades, or collapses as trajectories diverge.

Public intuition:

    not only positions changed
    structural behavior changed

Structural instability is a measurement target.

It is not a final decision.

Decision remains external.

---

## Boundary

These signals remain stress-test and measurement-level signals.

They are not:

- physical proof;
- semantic proof;
- universal chaos proof;
- downstream decision;
- security finding;
- cryptographic finding.

The boundary remains:

    measurement != inference != decision
