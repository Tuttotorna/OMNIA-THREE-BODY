# Boundary

OMNIA-THREE-BODY follows the ecosystem boundary:

    measurement != inference != decision

For this repository, the operational version is:

    dynamic stress test != physical proof != final decision

Decision remains external.

---

## Meaning

Stress test means:

    use a controlled dynamic setup to expose divergence or instability.

Measurement means:

    quantify or observe structural behavior under declared conditions.

Inference means:

    interpret the signal.

Decision means:

    act on the interpretation.

OMNIA-THREE-BODY creates dynamic stress-test artifacts.

It does not prove physics.

It does not decide.

---

## Allowed claims

OMNIA-THREE-BODY may claim:

- an initial state was defined;
- a perturbation was applied;
- trajectories diverged;
- structural instability appeared;
- an artifact should be validated;
- the case is useful as a stress test.

---

## Disallowed claims

OMNIA-THREE-BODY should not claim by itself:

- this proves physical truth;
- this proves a universal chaos law;
- this is semantically true;
- this is physically complete;
- this is safe;
- this is insecure;
- this downstream decision is correct.

---

## Rule

The output must preserve the distinction:

    dynamic stress test
    structural signal
    measurement artifact
    interpretation
    external decision
